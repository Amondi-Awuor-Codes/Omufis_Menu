Main Courses

Our main courses are the heroes of your stomach. Juicy grilled salmon, creamy chicken alfredo,
and our veggie burger that even meat-lovers secretly love—each plate comes with a side of “wow,
that’s amazing” and zero judgment if you lick it clean.

  • Grilled Salmon - Fresh salmon grilled to perfection with lemon butter sauce.
  • Chicken Alfredo - Creamy pasta with grilled chicken and parmesan cheese.
  • Veggie Burger - Homemade veggie patty served with fresh lettuce and tomato.

Desserts

Desserts at Omufisi Bites are basically tiny celebrations in a dish.

  • Chocolate Lava Cake - Warm chocolate cake with a gooey center, served with ice cream.
